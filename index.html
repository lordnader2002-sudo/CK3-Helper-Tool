Pro Tips & Strategy Guide

Advanced strategies to dominate Westeros.

⚔ Early Game Priorities

  • Immediately appoint your best characters to council positions
  • Max out your domain first — direct holdings generate far more income
  • Secure one strong alliance through marriage in your first year
  • Educate your heir personally or assign to best guardian
  • Start building economically productive buildings immediately
  • Investigate your council members for hooks early

👑 Vassal Management

  • Never let a single vassal control more than 25% of your realm strength
  • Give duchies to your own dynasty members where possible
  • Use Hooks to force vassals to support your decisions
  • The "Revoke Title" option is your nuclear button — use sparingly
  • Imprison and ransom ungrateful vassals to drain their gold
  • Keep vassal opinion above 0 — negative opinion spawns factions

💸 Economy Mastery

  • Cities generate more tax than castles — build them in fertile regions
  • Stewardship above 20 gives massive tax bonuses
  • Never go into debt — the interest kills your economy
  • Raid weaker neighbors early for startup gold
  • Westerlands gold mines are game-changing — capture them if possible
  • Invest in trade posts along the eastern coast for Essos trade income

⚔ Military Mastery

  • Quality beats quantity — Men-at-Arms are worth 5-10x levies
  • Never fight in enemy terrain without terrain-appropriate units
  • High Martial commanders dramatically improve battle outcomes
  • Siege with your main force, keep a flanking force to intercept relief
  • Supply attrition destroys armies — always check supply limits
  • In winter, short campaigns only — attrition is brutal

👁 Intrigue Essentials

  • Your Spymaster is your most important council member
  • Collect hooks obsessively — they're diplomatic currency
  • Always have a murder scheme ready against your top rival
  • Use Fabricate Hooks on powerful rulers before acting
  • Stress management is real — avoid mental break events
  • Keep your court physician skilled for health management

🔥 AGOT Specific

  • Dragon hatching is everything as Targaryen — prioritize events
  • Dragonglass is the only counter to White Walkers — stockpile it
  • Valyrian Steel swords are legendary artifacts — acquire them
  • Warg characters have unique map scouting abilities — exploit this
  • The Long Night event is existential — prepare 5+ years early
  • R'hllor faith gives resurrection mechanics — choose wisely

👥 Diplomacy & Alliances

  • Marry your heir to the daughter/son of your most dangerous neighbor
  • Alliance through marriage is the strongest guarantee of peace
  • Never declare war on two major powers simultaneously
  • Use "Invite to Court" to poach talented characters from enemies
  • Fabricated claims let you declare wars without Casus Belli shortages
  • Matrilineal marriages extend alliances without losing heirs

👑 Dynasty Power

  • Dynasty Legacy perks are long-term game changers — pick one and commit
  • Spread your dynasty members across Westeros as minor rulers
  • Dynasty Prestige opens unique interaction options
  • The Legitimacy mechanic in AGOT rewards established rule
  • Bastards can be legitimized — Targaryens especially should track this
  • Dynasty Renown bonuses stack — keeping a strong dynasty is self-reinforcing
